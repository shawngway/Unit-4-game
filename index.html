FoxEngineDecoder

You will be given a encrypted number at the start of the game.

There are four fox members below. By clicking on a member you will add a specific amount of points to your decryption points.

You win the game by matching your decryption points to the coded message, and you lose the game if your decryption points go above the coded message.

The Value of each member is hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each member.
